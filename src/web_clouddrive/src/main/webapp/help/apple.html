OS X


On OS X there are three clients. For all three you need to connect to https://rightfabric.net, use your username and your password to connect.


  1. - the built in OS X Finder. Works, but isn't as fast as the next two. Nothing to install though! To connect hit Cmd-K and go!

  2. - Transmit (see App Store or http://www.panic.com/transmit/). By far the fastest option, but alas, commercial. Does other protocols (and does them fast) as well. Connect with WebDAV SSL and go!

  3. - Cyberduck, see http://cyberduck.ch Open source, and between the previous two options in terms of speed. Again. connect WebDAV SSL and go!
