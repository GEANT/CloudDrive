iPhone, iPod Touch and iPad
For any of the following clients you should connect https://rightfabric.net , user your username and the password to connect. Often you use an option to connect to a server, and chose "Any (other) WebDAV server".


The clients:
  • iWork. Just connect to the webdav server, and you can browse and open your iWork files

  • WebDavNav. Just connect to the webdav server, and you can browse and open your files, or upload.

  • GoodReader (iPod, iPhone, iPad). Go to “Connect to Servers”, “Any other WebDAv server”, fill in the url, username and password, and you're good to go. Has a great sync option

  • Air Sharing Pro, works. Creating folders with spaces sometimes fails

  • DAV-E : not supported
